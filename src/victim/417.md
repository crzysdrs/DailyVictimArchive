Little peon  
 In the bush  
 Please forgive me  
 As I push  
 My right mouse button  
 At your feet  
 For gargoyle gluttons  
 To eat your meat  
 Suck the marrow  
 From your bones  
 Before I send them  
 Carting home  
 Your shriveled skin  
 So when I'm done  
 I'll conjure in  
 Another one

But in return  
 I'll offer thee  
 My earnest, honest  
 Sympathy  
 The peon life  
 Is hard indeed  
 You chop down wood  
 And then you're feed.
